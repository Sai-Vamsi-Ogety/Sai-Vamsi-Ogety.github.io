Reconstructing Noised Images of Fashion-MNIST Dataset using Autoencoders

Developed denoising autoencoders to reconstruct noisy images from the Fashion-MNIST dataset. Fine-tuned the autoencoder by varying hidden layer nodes, learning rate and noise level in the input images. Also developed stacked sparse autoencoders and performed greedy layer wise training with different levels of noise to classify the images. Achieved a final classification accuracy of 78% on the fine-tuned model.
